FAQ'S

What is my right size?

+

Since DRIP STUDIOS is designed around an oversized silhouette, we suggest sizing down if you’re uncertain about the fit. For instance, if you usually wear a Medium, opting for a Small would be a good choice with us. Once you’re familiar with the fit, you can always size up if you prefer a more relaxed or roomy look. We also recommend checking our size chart on the website, which includes detailed measurements for every size to help you find your ideal fit with confidence.

What are the care instructions?

+

All DRIP STUDIOS tees are suitable for machine washing. We recommend air-drying to maintain the fabric’s quality and shape. Please avoid dry cleaning. Always use cold water to prevent colour bleeding and minimize shrinkage. When ironing, ensure the print is not ironed directly to preserve its finish and longevity.
